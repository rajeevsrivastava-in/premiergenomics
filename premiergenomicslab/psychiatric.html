Premier Genomics™ can help you by determining how you may react to a prescription and if there is potential for any serious adverse side effects.

Over the last two decades physicians have been more active in screening for, and diagnosing patients with, mental health conditions. With an increasing prevalence of mental health disorders, many new medications have been brought to market to treat these conditions such as depression, anxiety, attention deficit hyperactivity disorder (ADHD), bipolar disorder, and schizophrenia. However, as with any medication, there may be both serious and non-serious side effects associated with their use.

This genetic test assesses and evaluates your ability to metabolize medications such as those mentioned above (also listed below). The information gathered from this test will help your provider make the right choice for you and your treatment and may be able to prevent both serious and non-serious side effects.

Here is a short list of psychiatric medications in which the FDA recommends genetic testing:
